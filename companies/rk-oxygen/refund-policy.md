## 1. Cancellation Policy

### 1.1 Order Cancellation Timeline
- **Within 2 hours of order placement**: Full cancellation allowed with no charges
- **2-24 hours after order placement**: Cancellation allowed with 10% processing fee
- **After 24 hours**: Cancellation subject to approval and may incur preparation charges
- **After dispatch**: Cancellation not permitted (return policy applies)

### 1.2 Special Orders and Custom Products
- Custom gas mixtures and special equipment orders cannot be cancelled once production begins
- Minimum 48-hour notice required for cancellation of special orders
- Cancellation fees may apply based on production stage

### 1.3 How to Cancel
1. Contact us immediately at **+91-7355755506** or **oxygen.rkgroup@gmail.com**
2. Provide order number and cancellation reason
3. Cancellation confirmation will be sent within 2 business hours
4. Refund processing will begin as per our refund timeline

## 2. Return Policy

### 2.1 Eligible Items for Return
✅ **Returnable Items:**
- Unopened and unused welding equipment (within 7 days)
- Defective or damaged products (within 3 days of delivery)
- Wrong items delivered (within 3 days of delivery)
- Unused gas cylinders with intact seals (within 24 hours)

❌ **Non-Returnable Items:**
- Used or opened gas cylinders (safety reasons)
- Custom gas mixtures
- Consumable items (welding rods, tips, etc.) once opened
- Equipment showing signs of use or damage
- Items without original packaging and documentation

### 2.2 Return Conditions
- Items must be in original condition with all accessories
- Original invoice and delivery receipt required
- Return authorization must be obtained before shipping
- Transportation and handling during return is buyer's responsibility
- Safety certifications must be maintained for gas cylinders

### 2.3 Return Process
1. **Request Return Authorization**: Contact us within the eligible timeframe
2. **Inspection**: Our team will inspect returned items for eligibility
3. **Documentation**: Provide photos of defective/damaged items if applicable
4. **Pickup/Delivery**: Arrange safe return of products to our facility
5. **Processing**: Refund will be initiated after successful inspection

## 3. Refund Policy

### 3.1 Refund Timeline
- **Order cancellation**: 3-5 business days
- **Defective/damaged products**: 5-7 business days after inspection
- **Wrong item delivered**: 3-5 business days after return confirmation
- **Cylinder returns**: Same day credit for unused cylinders with deposit

### 3.2 Refund Methods
- **Bank transfer**: Direct credit to original payment account
- **Cash**: For cash purchases (at branch locations only)
- **Store credit**: Option for faster processing and future purchases
- **Cylinder deposits**: Immediate credit upon return of undamaged cylinders

### 3.3 Refund Amounts
- **Full refund**: Defective products, wrong deliveries, cancelled orders (within policy)
- **Partial refund**: May apply for used or opened items (case-by-case basis)
- **Processing fees**: Deducted for late cancellations as specified
- **Shipping costs**: Non-refundable unless product was defective

## 4. Cylinder-Specific Policies

### 4.1 Gas Cylinder Returns
- **Unused cylinders**: Full refund within 24 hours of delivery
- **Deposit refund**: Immediate upon return of undamaged cylinder
- **Partially used cylinders**: No refund due to safety regulations
- **Exchange program**: Available for regular customers with credit accounts

### 4.2 Cylinder Damage
- **Customer responsibility**: Damage during use is not covered
- **Deposit forfeiture**: Damaged cylinders result in deposit loss
- **Safety inspection**: All returned cylinders undergo safety checks

## 5. Equipment and Accessories

### 5.1 Welding Equipment
- **7-day return window**: For unused equipment in original condition
- **Warranty coverage**: Separate from return policy (see warranty terms)
- **Installation services**: Non-refundable once installation is completed
- **Training costs**: Non-refundable after training delivery

### 5.2 Consumables and Spare Parts
- **No returns**: Consumable items cannot be returned once opened
- **Defective items**: Replacement provided for manufacturing defects
- **Bulk orders**: Special return terms may apply (contact for details)

## 6. Special Circumstances

### 6.1 Emergency Orders
- **Priority delivery**: Cancellation charges may be higher
- **Weekend/holiday delivery**: Additional fees non-refundable
- **Expedited processing**: Rush charges are non-refundable

### 6.2 Contract Customers
- **Custom terms**: Contract customers may have different policies
- **Volume discounts**: May affect refund calculations
- **Credit terms**: Special refund procedures for credit accounts

## 7. Dispute Resolution

### 7.1 Complaint Process
1. Contact our customer service team immediately
2. Provide detailed information and supporting documentation
3. Allow 24-48 hours for initial response
4. Escalation to management if required
5. Final resolution within 7 business days

### 7.2 Quality Assurance
- All products undergo quality checks before dispatch
- Safety certifications maintained for all gas products
- Equipment testing performed as per industry standards
- Customer satisfaction surveys conducted regularly
