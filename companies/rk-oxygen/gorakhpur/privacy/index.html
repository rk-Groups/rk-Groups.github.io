Information We Collect

At RK Oxygen Gorakhpur, we collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you:

  • Place orders for industrial gases or welding supplies
  • Contact us for quotes, delivery scheduling, or technical support
  • Register for our services or create an account
  • Participate in surveys or provide feedback
  • Visit our physical location or attend business meetings

The types of information we may collect include:

  • Business name, contact person, and billing/shipping addresses
  • Phone numbers and email addresses for order communication
  • Order history, preferences, and purchase patterns
  • Technical requirements for gas types and equipment
  • Payment information (processed securely through authorized channels)

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Process and fulfill your orders for industrial gases and supplies
  • Provide accurate quotations and delivery scheduling
  • Offer technical support and safety guidance
  • Maintain accurate billing and payment records
  • Ensure compliance with safety regulations and industry standards
  • Improve our services and customer experience
  • Communicate important updates about orders or safety information

Safety First: We prioritize safety in all our operations. Your contact information helps us provide timely safety updates, emergency notifications, and proper handling instructions for hazardous materials.

Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties except in the following circumstances:

  • With your explicit consent for specific business purposes
  • To comply with legal obligations, court orders, or regulatory requirements
  • To protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of our customers and employees
  • With trusted service providers who assist us in operating our business (under strict confidentiality agreements)
  • For delivery coordination with authorized logistics partners
  • For payment processing through secure financial institutions

Data Security

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your information:

  • Physical security measures at our branch location
  • Secure data storage and access controls
  • Regular security audits and employee training
  • Encrypted communication channels for sensitive information
  • Proper disposal procedures for physical and digital records
  • Compliance with data protection regulations

While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but are committed to protecting your data using industry best practices.

Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about your business
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your information (subject to legal and business requirements)

  • Complaints: File complaints about our data handling practices

To exercise these rights, please contact our branch directly using the information provided below.
