Safety & Handling

Hazardous Materials: Industrial gases are classified as hazardous materials. All shipments comply with national and international transportation regulations including proper labeling, packaging, and documentation.

Safety Measures

  • DOT/IMDG certified cylinders and containers
  • Proper valve protection and pressure testing
  • Temperature-controlled transportation when required
  • Emergency response protocols and spill containment
  • Trained personnel for loading and unloading

Customer Responsibilities

  • Provide safe access for delivery vehicles
  • Ensure proper storage facilities are available
  • Follow safety guidelines for handling delivered products
  • Report any damage or issues immediately upon delivery

Delivery Terms & Conditions
